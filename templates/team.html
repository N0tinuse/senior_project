Team


Govinda Dasu came up with the idea for Learning Dollars while pondering the following hypothetical: If everyone in the world were educated, would poverty and unemployment exist? He wagered no. There is always demand for labor, but the catch is that that labor often requires rare skills. If skills could be easily learned and applied through cohesive and integrated web modules, labor markets would be more liquid and wealth could reach impoverished hands. At Learning Dollars, Dasu focuses on business and web development.

Dasu is a member of the Stanford Class of 2014 (Computer Science Major, Artificial Intelligence Track) and Harker Class of 2012.
